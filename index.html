GETTING STARTED
1. Tap ▶ START PROBE on splash screen
2. Grant microphone permission when prompted
3. Scope begins capturing from mic or AUX jack
4. Use OSC tab sliders to adjust zoom and gain
5. Open ☰ menu for trigger, calibration and settings
OSCILLOSCOPE TAB
Time-domain waveform. X = time · Y = amplitude

ZOOM — zooms time axis
GAIN — amplifies signal vertically
T/DIV — manual timebase (◀ ▶ to step)
V/DIV — manual voltage scale (◀ ▶ to step)
FFT SPECTRUM TAB
Frequency content. X = Hz · Y = amplitude

Green = low · Yellow = medium · Red = high energy
Dominant peak = reported FREQ measurement
TRIGGER SYSTEM
AUTO — always displays even without trigger
NORMAL — only updates on trigger event
SINGLE — captures once then freezes

RISE ↑ — rising edge crossing upper threshold
FALL ↓ — falling edge crossing lower threshold
BOTH — triggers on either edge

Use MANUAL LEVEL to type exact threshold in volts
SIGNAL DETECTION
Classifies using 3 algorithms:

Crest Factor (peak÷RMS):
Sine≈1.41 · Triangle≈1.73 · Square≈1.0

Clip Ratio — high % at min/max = Square
Slope Asymmetry — Sawtooth unequal rise/fall
AUX JACK SAFETY
⚠️ NEVER connect mains voltage to AUX jack
⚠️ Max safe input: <1Vpp audio-level only
✅lower the voltage to safe levels (you will decide your method)
